How Eldora works

Eldora makes trip planning easier. Eldora recommends routes, analyzes risk and makes informed packing recommendations for your adventures.

Recommending Routes

Eldora recommends hiking, cycling or runner routes, based on where you'd like to go and what kind of experience you're looking for.

Analyzing Risk

Think about the risks you analyze when preparing for a trip: what's a reasonable worst-case weather scenario? Will I lose cell service (and in which parts of the trip)? Where are my water sources? Where is the snowline? Eldora analyzes those risks for you.

Packing recommendations

After Eldora analyzes the risks associated with your route, we make informed packing suggestions to help you have a safe, less stressful trip.
